Robot See, Robot Do.
Train robots with the wave of a hand.
Vision
We're here to make working with robots as simple as showing them what you want done. Forget spending hours struggling with complicated setups and confusing instructions.
Adaptive
You never stop learning. Neither should your tools. Our software allows robots to continuously learn new motions. You want something new? Just show the robot.
Intuitive
This is what using the technology of the future should feel like. Teach the robot just as you would a person. Let the robot take care of the rest.
Robust
The real world is messy. Our intelligence is constantly scanning its environment for obstacles to plan around and changes to remember. It’s time to get robots out of the lab.
Philosophy
Love What You Do.
Share Prosperity.
Chase Greatness.

We believe these are the rules for a fulfilling life. In our aim to build a better world, these are the rules we follow and the rules we want people to believe are worth following for themselves.

These are also principles that we believe are lacking in the world. And it’s not hard to see why. People can not simply choose to chase greatness because they are having to work to survive. The prosperity resulting from technology is still primarily restricted to those who have the time and capital to treat it as a toy project. It’s impossible to love what you do when you’re not even choosing what to do.

It’s easy to say that if the world were a better place, everyone would obviously choose to live this way. There’s only one way to know for sure. We want to put the power to choose back in your hands. We want to help you chase greatness.
